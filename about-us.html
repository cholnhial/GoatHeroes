Our little story

We want to revolutionise how landscape is managed. Our planet is increasingingly facing dramatic climate crisis and it would go a long way to try And minimise the effects of climate change. Traditional ways of mowing lawns and doing landscaping use huge amounts of fossil fuels which add To the issue. GoatHeroes has a solution. Send out little heroes of goats to go and cut your grass for you or eat weeds in your garden. These little Companions will leave the whole family happy and having fun.

The process is simple once you’ve made your booking and put in your property address and your contact details and the time you’d like us to deliver the goats We will give you a contact and you can either pay on phone or later on your premise. The goats are carefully selected for your premise. We works with hundreds of farmers Across Australia to acquire the goats. Every goat has a special tracking tag and so they won’t get lost. We recommend properties with a closed fence and a gate. This Is because we take extra precautions so that our goats do not go missing. We service homes, businesses, farms and etc.

We are new and are always willing to take feedback from our customers so that we can improve.

We started in DECO1400 a course at UQ now this is a legit business, but it was just an assignment.
